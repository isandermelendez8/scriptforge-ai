Common Questions

How do I activate my license? ▼
Download the ScriptForge AI desktop app, login with Discord, and enter your license key. Your key is linked to your Discord account and is delivered via Discord DM after purchase.
Do I need my own API keys? ▼
No — ScriptForge AI comes with built-in AI access. Optionally you can add your own Claude, OpenAI or Gemini API keys in Settings for unlimited personal usage beyond your plan limits.
What is the Lifetime plan? ▼
Pay $49 once and get everything forever — no monthly fees, all future tools included, unlimited generations, and all 3 AI models. It never expires and includes all updates we ship.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes. Cancel anytime from within the app or via our Discord server. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.
Which FiveM frameworks are supported? ▼
ESX, QBCore, ox_core, vRP, and Standalone (no framework). The converter supports all combinations including vRP to ESX/QBCore.
Is the app available on Mac and Linux? ▼
Currently the app is Windows-first. Mac and Linux builds are in progress and will be available soon. The web version works on all platforms.
